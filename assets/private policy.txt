Privacy Policy
1. Introduction
Moon Light Hotel & Bakery ("we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our guests' personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal data we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to safeguard it.

2. Information We Collect
Personal Information: Includes name, address, phone number, email address, payment information, and government-issued identification.
Booking Information: Reservation details, stay dates, room preferences, and special requests.
Bakery Orders: Information related to bakery orders, including order details and preferences.
Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

3. How We Use Your Information
Booking and Reservations: To process and manage your reservations and stays.
Payment Processing: To process payments for reservations and bakery orders.
Communication: To communicate with you regarding your stay, reservations, and bakery orders.
Personalization: To personalize your experience and improve our services.
Legal Requirements: To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure
Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our business, such as payment processors and IT support.
Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety, or the rights and safety of others.
Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the business transaction.

5. Data Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. These measures include encryption, access controls, and secure data storage.

6. Data Retention
We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

7. Your Rights
Access and Correction: You have the right to access and correct your personal information.
Deletion: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and contractual restrictions.
Objection and Restriction: You have the right to object to or request the restriction of the processing of your personal information under certain circumstances.
Data Portability: You have the right to request the transfer of your personal information to another organization.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies
Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and gather usage data. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

9. Third-Party Links
Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these websites.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes and update the effective date at the top of this policy.
